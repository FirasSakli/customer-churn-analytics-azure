# Business Case: Customer Churn Prediction

## Background
Customer churn is a key challenge for subscription-based and telecom companies. Acquiring new customers
is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones.

## Objective
The objective of this project is to:
- Predict the probability of customer churn
- Identify key drivers behind churn behavior
- Enable proactive retention actions

## Business Impact
Reducing churn by even a small percentage can lead to significant revenue savings.
The output of this project is intended for use by:
- Customer success teams
- Marketing and retention teams
- Management decision-makers

## Success Criteria
- A validated churn prediction model (ROC-AUC as primary metric)
- Interpretable model outputs
- Clear business recommendations
